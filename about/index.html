About
Switchonyms is a chaotic party game of words and guesses for 4+ people.
Launch
Every game has a unique 4-character id. Just go to the homepage to start a new one - or join your friend's!

The game is divided into 3 rounds: nouns, verbs, and adjectives.
Gameplay
Every round, 2 players will each have a secret word. But the longer you have a word, the more points you lose - so give good clues so someone will guess it.

If you don't have a word, listen to those clues to guess one. You get a whole bunch of points - but now you have a word too!
