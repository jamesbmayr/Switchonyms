About
Switchonyms is a chaotic party game of words and guesses for 4+ people.
Launch
Every game has a unique 4-character id. Just go to the homepage to start a new one - or join your friend's!

The game is divided into 3 rounds: nouns, verbs, and adjectives. Each round has two phases: matching and guessing.
Gameplay
In the matching phase, players race to match up with an opponent who has the same secret word they do. Everyone has 2 words to try - but finding a match is harder than it sounds!

In the guessing phase, 2 players will each have a secret word. Get points by guessing a secret word based on the players' clues - but lose points for every second that nobody guesses your word!
